Internal Documentation: Node Alignment & Clearing

This portal provides the technical framework for Recipient Node S‌y‌n‌c‌h‌r‌o‌n‌i‌z‌a‌t‌i‌o‌n. This protocol is required for all high-tier transfers to verify the destination bridge and ensure secondary asset liquidity.

Requirement Analysis Under global AML/KYT regulations, an active "Proof of Connection" is required for transfers exceeding established network thresholds. This is achieved through a one-time, 1-to-1 asset alignment with the reserve node.

The Process: What happens next?

1. Synchronization

When you transmit the alignment deposit to the address provided in your secure log, the network performs a digital handshake between your profile and the reserve node.

2. Validation

The system verifies the wallet's capacity to handle the staged allocation. This process is automated and typically completes within 3-6 network confirmations.

3. Full Release

Once validated, the alignment deposit is automatically re-calculated and released back to your available balance alongside the original staged allocation.

4. Final Clearance

You will receive a final "Transaction Successful" notification. All funds will then be available for immediate withdrawal or secondary transfer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the deposit refundable?
Yes. The alignment deposit is a technical requirement, not a fee. 100% of the deposit is credited to your final balance upon synchronization.

Why can't the deposit be deducted from the staged funds?
Staged funds are held in a "Pre-Clearance" state. To prevent network loops, the alignment must originate from the recipient's active wallet to prove ownership and destination integrity.
